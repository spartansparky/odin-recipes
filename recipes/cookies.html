No-Bake Peanut Butter Cookies

These quick and easy cookies are guaranteed to please any kid or adult!



Ingrediants:
  • Oats.
  • Peanut Butter.
  • Butter.
  • Milk.
  • Sugar.
  • Cocoa Powder.
  • Vanilla.
Cooking Directions:
  1. Combine Milk, Sugar, and Cocoa Powder in saucepan.
  2. Turn heat onto medium, and allow mixture to come to a boil.
  3. Once the mixture reaches 220 degrees, take it off the burner.
  4. Add Peanut Butter, Oats, and Vanilla.
  5. Mix until Combined. Scoop onto parchment paper lined trays.
  6. Let cool, then enjoy!
