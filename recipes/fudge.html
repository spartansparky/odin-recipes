Peanut Butter Fudge

This simple 3-ingrediant recipe is sure to be a hit at your next party!



Ingrediants:
  • Peanut Butter.
  • Butter.
  • Powdered Sugar.
    • < /ul> Cooking Directions:
    1. Combine Butter and Peanut Butter in a bowl and microwave until melted.
    2. Mix Powdered Sugar into the mixture.
    3. Enjoy!
